The Ultimate Student Learning Platform & Productivity Suite

MARGDARSHAK is a comprehensive student management system designed to help students across the globe achieve academic excellence. We provide a suite of free, high-quality tools to manage grades, tasks, and study schedules.

Core Academic Tools

Our platform replaces fragmented apps with a unified dashboard.

1. Scientific Calculator

A robust online calculator for trigonometry, calculus, and algebra. Perform complex calculations without leaving your study dashboard. Optimized for speed and accuracy.

2. Pomodoro Study Timer

Customizable timer based on the Pomodoro Technique. Set 25-minute focus intervals followed by 5-minute breaks to maximize retention and prevent burnout.

3. Advanced Grade Tracking

Input your assignment scores, quizzes, and exams to automatically calculate and monitor your GPA. Visualize your academic progress over the semester.

4. Task Manager

A Kanban-style board to organize homework and project deadlines. Move tasks from "To Do" to "In Progress" to "Done".
